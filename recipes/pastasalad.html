Easy Rainbow Pasta Salad

Description

This rainbow pasta salad is really quick and easy to make. Great for picnics and summer parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package tri-colored pasta assortment
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Drain the pasta and rinse it in cold water.
  4. Combine the tomatoes, cucumber, onion, cooled pasta, and Italian dressing in a large bowl.
  5. Toss everything together until well coated.
  6. Cover and refrigerate the pasta salad for at least 1 hour or overnight before serving.
